# Musasabi Evolution Modules Directive

## Purpose
Implement the next generation internal operating modules for Musasabi OS using mock services first.

## Modules
1. AI Operating Manual
2. AI Skill Marketplace
3. AI Sandbox
4. AI Incident Center
5. AI Meeting Room
6. AI Simulation Engine
7. AI Recruiting
8. AI Upgrade Manager
9. AI Health Center
10. AI Memory Timeline
11. AI Command Console
12. AI Builder Department (Musasabi Evolution Lab)

## Responsibilities
- AI Operating Manual: auto-generate and maintain department operating manuals.
- AI Skill Marketplace: share reusable AI skills across departments.
- AI Sandbox: isolated testing for new features, departments and AI employees.
- AI Incident Center: incident detection, recovery planning and post-incident review.
- AI Meeting Room: scheduled executive meeting summaries and action tracking.
- AI Simulation Engine: simulate organizational and KPI changes.
- AI Recruiting: recommend creation or reassignment of AI employees.
- AI Upgrade Manager: evaluate new AI model versions before adoption.
- AI Health Center: monitor AI employee operational health and recommend maintenance.
- AI Memory Timeline: searchable organizational history.
- AI Command Console: natural-language operational commands.
- AI Builder Department: design and propose improvements to Musasabi OS itself.

## Rules
Use mock data first. No production external connections. No secrets. Integrate with Company Brain, Musasabi DNA, governance, audit and executive dashboards. Update tests, README and CLAUDE_RESPONSE.md.

## Completion Criteria
Each module has a mock service, UI panel or documented architecture and integrates with the existing dashboard and governance model.
