# Phase 4: Autonomous Enterprise

Directive ID: D-20260708-005

## Goal
Operate Musasabi OS as a self-improving AI enterprise while keeping human approval for high-impact actions.

## Objectives
- AI PM coordinates departments end-to-end.
- Automatic issue generation from improvement proposals.
- Continuous implementation pipeline with review gates.
- Business KPI dashboards for every department.
- AI employee performance analytics and optimization.
- Knowledge sharing across all departments.
- Daily and end-of-day executive reports.

## Safety Rules
- Human approval remains mandatory for production deployments, financial actions, legal actions and external communications unless explicitly configured otherwise.
- Preserve audit logs for all autonomous decisions.
- Never expose secrets or bypass approval policies.

## Completion Criteria
- End-to-end autonomous workflow is demonstrated using mock and approved integrations.
- AI PM manages prioritization and execution queues.
- Executive dashboard summarizes company status.
- Documentation, tests and CLAUDE_RESPONSE.md are updated.

## Next Step
After Phase 3 is complete, begin Phase 4 by enabling autonomous optimization, reporting and continuous improvement while respecting all approval policies.
