# Phase 5: Musasabi OS Product Launch

Directive ID: D-20260708-006

## Goal
Transform Musasabi OS from an internal AI platform into a commercial SaaS product while preserving the modular architecture built in earlier phases.

## Objectives
- Multi-tenant organization support.
- User, company and administrator management.
- Subscription and feature-tier architecture (mock first).
- Customer onboarding and setup wizard.
- Licensing and usage controls.
- Product website and demo environment.
- Documentation portal and customer support workflows.
- Security, audit logging and operational monitoring.

## Safety Rules
- Financial transactions remain mock until explicitly approved.
- Customer data isolation is mandatory.
- Secrets and production credentials must never be committed.

## Completion Criteria
- Commercial product architecture is available.
- Tenant-aware authentication and authorization design is implemented.
- Plan-based feature management is available.
- Documentation, tests, README and CLAUDE_RESPONSE.md are updated.

## Next Step
After Phase 4 is complete, begin Product Launch implementation in incremental milestones, validating each capability before enabling it for production customers.
