# Musasabi World Directive

## Purpose
Create the long-term product vision where a user can generate and operate an AI company from templates.

## Concept
Musasabi World is the environment where new AI companies, business units, departments, AI executives, AI employees, dashboards, workflows, knowledge bases and reports can be generated from a single business idea.

## Core Experience
User enters a business idea or selects a template.
Musasabi OS generates:
- AI CEO structure
- Executive team
- Business unit
- Department map
- AI employee roster
- KPI dashboard
- Workflow templates
- Company Brain workspace
- Musasabi DNA profile
- Knowledge Vault folders
- Report templates
- Audit monitoring
- Mock operating data

## Required Screens
- Create AI Company wizard
- Business template selector
- Generated organization preview
- KPI preview
- Workflow preview
- Launch confirmation mock
- Generated company dashboard

## First Use Cases
- MEISHI-TUBE company workspace
- SaaS company workspace
- Sales agency company workspace
- Publishing company workspace

## Rules
Start with mock generation only. Do not create production accounts, external connections, billing, banking, or secrets. Follow governance, audit and approval rules.

## Completion Criteria
- User can create a mock AI company workspace from a template.
- Generated workspace appears in dashboard.
- AI CEO avatar can summarize the created company.
- README and CLAUDE_RESPONSE.md are updated in Japanese.
