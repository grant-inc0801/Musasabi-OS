# Musasabi Next Core Modules

Directive ID: D-20260709-002

## Purpose
Add the next core modules needed to strengthen Musasabi OS as an AI company operating system.

## Modules to Implement

1. AI Constitution
- Top-level operating principles.
- Human approval required for changes.
- Referenced by AI CEO, executives, departments and AI employees.

2. AI Mission Control
- AI CEO command screen.
- Shows company status, executives, departments, priorities and approvals.

3. AI Situation Room
- Real-time operations view.
- Shows KPI, tasks, alerts, pending items and executive messages.

4. AI Digital Twin
- Mock simulation of the company.
- Estimates impact of resource changes, budget changes and department changes.

5. AI Relationship Graph
- Connects people, departments, projects, customers, documents, issues and workflows.

6. AI Memory Engine
- Stores AI employee experience, lessons, success cases and improvement history.

7. AI Customer Brain
- Customer knowledge layer for sales and support.
- Uses mock customer data first.

8. AI Quality Assurance
- Cross-department quality checks for UI, tests, docs and workflows.

9. AI Security Center
- Permission, secret handling policy, access review and connector safety overview.

10. AI Cost Optimizer
- Tracks model usage, API usage, compute cost and recommends savings.

11. AI Competitor Center
- Tracks competitors, differentiation, strengths and weaknesses.

12. AI Innovation Lab
- Generates new service ideas, improvement ideas and experiment proposals.

## Implementation Rules
- Use mock data first.
- Extend existing architecture.
- Keep desktop app stable.
- Do not add production external connections.
- Do not require secrets.
- Update tests, README and CLAUDE_RESPONSE.md.
