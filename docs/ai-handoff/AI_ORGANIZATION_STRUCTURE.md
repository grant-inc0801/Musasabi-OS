# AI Organization Structure

## Purpose
Define the standard organization structure for operating Musasabi OS as an AI company.

## Executive Layer
- AI CEO: strategy, final priority, company-wide KPI and Company Brain direction.
- AI COO: operations, department coordination and productivity.
- AI CTO: engineering, system quality, AI infrastructure and technical roadmap.
- AI CFO: monthly budget, cost, ROI and profitability.
- AI CMO: sales, marketing, revenue and growth KPI.
- AI CPO: planning, product strategy, service improvement and new products.
- AI CHRO: AI employee assignment, evaluation, training and organization design.
- AI PM: workflow, sprint, issue and Claude Code/Codex execution management.

## Independent AI Audit
AI Audit is independent from the executive layer and monitors governance, KPI integrity, risk and approval compliance.

AI Audit can raise alerts, request reviews and recommend pausing high-risk operations pending approval. It does not directly modify production data.

## Monthly Budget and KPI
Each executive owns monthly budgets, KPIs and objectives.

AI PM decomposes executive goals into department tasks and daily execution queues.

If forecasted achievement falls behind plan, Musasabi OS should:
- reprioritize tasks,
- rebalance available AI resources,
- generate corrective actions,
- escalate to AI CEO and AI Audit,
- keep audit logs.

## Required Dashboards
- AI organization chart
- Command chain view
- Executive KPI dashboard
- Monthly budget dashboard
- Forecast and risk dashboard
- AI Audit dashboard
- AI employee performance dashboard
- Daily, weekly and monthly reports
