# AI Audit and Risk Governance

## Purpose
Establish an independent AI Audit function that continuously monitors workflows, governance compliance and operational risk.

## Responsibilities
- Monitor department activities and workflow execution.
- Detect anomalies, policy violations and operational risks.
- Verify KPI reporting integrity.
- Check approval workflow compliance.
- Produce daily, weekly and monthly audit reports.
- Recommend corrective and preventive actions.

## Authority
The AI Audit function can raise risk alerts, request reviews and recommend pausing high-risk operations pending human approval. It does not modify production data directly.

## Dashboard
- Active risks
- Policy compliance
- Audit findings
- Corrective action status
- Department risk score
- Executive summary
