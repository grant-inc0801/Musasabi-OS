# AI Business Factory Directive

## Purpose
Enable Musasabi OS to launch and operate new business units using standardized templates.

## Business Unit Template
When a new business is created, generate a Business Unit with:
- AI Business Director
- Sales Team
- Marketing Team
- Development Team
- Operations Team
- Customer Success Team
- Finance Support
- AI Audit liaison

## Automatic Provisioning
For each new business unit automatically create:
- Department structure
- KPI dashboard
- Executive dashboard integration
- Workflow templates
- Company Brain workspace
- Knowledge repository
- Reporting templates
- Risk monitoring
- Mock operating data

## Governance
Business Units report through AI COO to AI CEO. They follow Musasabi Constitution, governance and audit policies.

## Initial Target
Use MEISHI-TUBE as the first Business Unit template.

## Rules
Use mock implementations first. Extend existing architecture and documentation.
