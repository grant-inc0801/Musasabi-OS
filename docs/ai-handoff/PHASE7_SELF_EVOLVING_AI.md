# Phase 7: Self-Evolving AI

Directive ID: D-20260708-008

Goal: Enable Musasabi AI to continuously analyze its own operation and propose improvements while keeping human approval for impactful changes.

Objectives:
- Autonomous improvement proposals
- AI employee performance analysis
- Workflow optimization suggestions
- Automatic draft Issue generation
- Company Brain evolution
- Knowledge quality scoring

Safety:
- High-impact changes require human approval.
- Preserve complete audit logs.
- Never bypass approval policies.

Completion Criteria:
- Improvement proposals are generated automatically.
- AI PM prioritizes proposals.
- Draft implementation plans are produced.
- Documentation and tests are updated.
