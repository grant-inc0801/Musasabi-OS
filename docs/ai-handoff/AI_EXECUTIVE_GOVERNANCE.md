# AI Executive Governance

## Executive Structure
AI CEO, AI COO, AI CTO, AI CFO, AI CMO, AI CPO, AI CHRO and AI PM.

## Monthly Budget & KPI Framework
Each executive owns monthly budgets, KPIs and departmental objectives.

Rules:
- Budgets cascade from AI CEO.
- AI PM converts goals into department tasks.
- Daily progress reviews.
- If performance falls behind target, automatically reprioritize work, rebalance available AI resources, recommend corrective actions and escalate to AI CEO.
- Governance approval is required for major strategic changes.

## Continuous Improvement
The system continuously forecasts target achievement, identifies risks, recommends operational improvements and updates priorities. AI roles are evaluated and workflows are optimized based on performance metrics.

## Dashboard
Show monthly targets, current performance, forecast, budget usage, risks, recommended actions and executive summaries.
